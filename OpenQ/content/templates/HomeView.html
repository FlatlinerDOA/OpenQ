Welcome to OpenQ

What is OpenQ?

OpenQ is a service for storing and distributing your messages. That's it, plain and simple. What's unique about OpenQ is how simple and flexible OpenQ is to use.

  • Use it as way to backup and distribute your data
  • Use it as a message bus for your applications to scale out with
  • Use it as an event store to build an Event Sourced CQRS based system on top of

Push based Subscriptions

Setup a subscription to one of your web sites and get OpenQ to push your messages to you.

Get started for free... only pay if you want to.

OpenQ has a free tier that is throughput limited. If you need more performance you can dial up your throughput instantly.
